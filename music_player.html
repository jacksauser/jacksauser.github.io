Music Player

This is the frontend for a mobile Music Player applicaion. Written in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

There are three main pages: Albums, Tracks, and Player.

The 'Albums' and 'Tracks' pages are written in HTML/CSS. Links are used to navigate between the pages and select specific albums/tracks to play.

On the 'Player' page HTML/CSS is used for design and layout, and JavaScript is used to control the state of the player. This includes playing/pausing/skipping, volumn up/down, and displaying current song duration.
